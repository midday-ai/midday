When we started Midday, the goal was simple: take the admin off your plate. Match receipts, track expenses, give you clarity.

<br />

That worked. But as more founders started using Midday as their daily system, something shifted. They weren't just managing receipts—they were running entire businesses through it. Sending invoices, tracking client hours, reconciling every transaction, exporting clean books to their accountant. Midday had quietly become the backbone.

<br />

Today we're making that official. Midday is the business stack for modern founders.

<br />

## What Changed

We've restructured the product around the workflows that matter most to founders running real businesses:

<br />

- **Invoicing** — Create, send, and track invoices. Get paid faster with online payments.
- **Transactions** — Every payment in and out, synced from 25,000+ banks, categorized automatically.
- **Inbox** — Receipts pulled from email, uploads, and integrations—matched to the right transactions.
- **Time Tracking** — Track billable hours, link them to projects and customers, generate invoices directly.
- **Customers** — Revenue per customer, payment history, outstanding invoices—all in one view.
- **Exports** — Clean, accountant-ready records. Transactions, receipts, and categories ready to sync or export.

<br />

These aren't separate tools bolted together. They're one connected system where data flows naturally between features. An invoice gets paid, the transaction appears, the receipt matches, and your books stay clean without you lifting a finger.

<br />

## AI as Infrastructure

Earlier this year we launched [MCP support](/mcp)—connecting Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Raycast, and others directly to your business data. The response was overwhelming.

<br />

Founders started asking their AI tools questions like "What's our runway?" or "Which invoices are overdue?" and getting real answers from real numbers. No copy-pasting, no exporting CSVs, no context-switching.

<br />

With today's update, AI integrations are a core part of the stack. Your business data becomes a foundation that any AI tool can build on. We now support:

<br />

- [Claude](/mcp/claude), [ChatGPT](/mcp/chatgpt), [Perplexity](/mcp/perplexity) for analysis and insights
- [Cursor](/mcp/cursor) and [OpenCode](/mcp/opencode) for developers tracking time while they code
- [Raycast](/mcp/raycast) for quick access without leaving your workflow
- [Zapier](/mcp/zapier), [n8n](/mcp/n8n), and [Make](/mcp/make) for automation

<br />
