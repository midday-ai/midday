Your business now fits in a chat window. Connect Midday to iMessage, WhatsApp, Slack, or Telegram and do real work — send invoices, match receipts, chase payments, check your numbers — without opening a dashboard.

<br />

We built this because the best business tool is the one you're already using. For most founders, that's a messaging app. So we brought Midday there.

<br />

## What you can do

<br />

- **Get invoices paid** — Midday notifies you when an invoice is overdue. Reply "send reminder" and a payment link goes out to your client. Midday watches for the payment and lets you know when it lands.

- **Create invoices from a message** — Type something like "invoice Linear for 40 hours at $150/h" and Midday drafts a $6,000 invoice. Review it in the chat preview, say "send now", and it's delivered.

- **Match receipts instantly** — Snap a photo of a receipt and send it. Midday extracts the merchant, amount, date, and card — then matches it to the right bank transaction automatically.

- **Check your numbers** — Ask "what are my latest transactions?" or "flag anything unusual" and get a summary. Midday pulls from your live data and highlights trends like a 12% increase in software spend.

<br />

## Works where you already are

<br />

Four platforms, same capabilities:

- **[iMessage](/chat/imessage)** — Works through Sendblue. Get push notifications on your lock screen and reply from anywhere.
- **[WhatsApp](/chat/whatsapp)** — Scan a QR code and you're connected. Works on any device.
- **[Slack](/chat/slack)** — Install to your workspace. Use DMs or channels.
- **[Telegram](/chat/telegram)** — Add the Midday bot. Lightweight and fast.

<br />

Every action you take in chat — sending an invoice, matching a receipt, tracking time — syncs back to your Midday dashboard. Your books stay clean and your data stays in one place, whether you're working from the app or a group chat.

<br />

## Get started

<br />

1. Go to **Apps** in your Midday dashboard
2. Connect your preferred chat platform
3. Start messaging

<br />

That's it. No configuration, no learning curve. Just text Midday like you'd text a colleague.
