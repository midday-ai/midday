## Notification types

### Invoice notifications

- **Invoice paid**: When a customer pays an invoice
- **Invoice viewed**: When a customer opens an invoice
- **Invoice overdue**: When an invoice passes its due date

### Transaction notifications

- **Large transactions**: Alerts for transactions above a threshold
- **New transactions**: Daily or weekly summaries of new transactions

### Bank notifications

- **Connection issues**: When a bank connection needs attention
- **Low balance**: When account balance drops below a threshold

### Team notifications

- **New member joined**: When an invited member accepts
- **Member activity**: Summaries of team activity

## Delivery methods

Choose how to receive notifications:

- **Email**: Traditional email notifications
- **In-app**: Notifications within Midday

## Frequency settings

For some notifications, set frequency:

- **Real-time**: As events happen
- **Daily digest**: Once per day summary
- **Weekly digest**: Weekly summary

## Tips

- Enable invoice paid notifications to know immediately when you get paid
- Set low balance alerts to avoid overdrafts
- Use digests for less urgent items to reduce inbox noise
- Review settings periodically as your needs change
