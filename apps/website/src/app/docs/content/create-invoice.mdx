Midday makes it easy to create professional invoices that look great and help you get paid faster.

## Create a new invoice

1. Go to [Invoices](https://app.midday.ai/invoices)
2. Click **Create invoice**
3. Fill in the details (see below)
4. Choose **Send** to email immediately, or **Save as draft**

## Invoice details

### Customer

Select an existing customer or create a new one. You'll need:

- Customer name
- Email address (for sending)
- Optional: Address, VAT number, contact person

### Line items

Add what you're billing for:

- **Description**: What the item or service is
- **Quantity**: Number of units
- **Price**: Unit price
- **Tax**: Optional tax rate

Add as many line items as needed. The total calculates automatically.

### Invoice settings

- **Invoice number**: Auto-generated, but you can customize
- **Issue date**: When the invoice is created
- **Due date**: When payment is expected
- **Currency**: Defaults to your base currency, can change per invoice
- **Notes**: Optional message to your customer

## Sending options

### Send by email

Click **Send** to email the invoice directly to your customer. They receive:

- A professional email with your branding
- A PDF attachment of the invoice
- A link to pay online (if you've enabled online payments)

### Share a link

Copy the invoice link to share via any channel. Your customer can view and pay online.

### Download PDF

Download a PDF to send manually or keep for your records.

## After sending

Track invoice status from the [Invoices](https://app.midday.ai/invoices) list:

- **Draft**: Not sent yet
- **Sent**: Delivered to customer
- **Viewed**: Customer opened the invoice
- **Paid**: Payment received
- **Overdue**: Past due date, not yet paid

[Learn more about tracking invoice status →](/docs/track-invoice-status)

## Tips

- Set up your logo and business details in [Settings → Invoice](https://app.midday.ai/settings) for professional branding
- Use [recurring invoices](/docs/set-up-recurring-invoice) for retainer clients
- Enable [online payments](/docs/accept-online-payments) to get paid faster
