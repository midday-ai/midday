Midday is your business's financial operating system. It brings together banking, invoicing, time tracking, and bookkeeping preparation in one place—so you can spend less time on admin and more time on the work that matters.

## Who is Midday for?

Midday is built for:

- **Freelancers** who need to track income, send invoices, and stay on top of expenses
- **Solopreneurs** running their own business and wanting clear financial visibility
- **Small agencies** managing multiple clients, projects, and team members
- **Consultants** who bill by the hour and need to track time accurately

If you're tired of juggling spreadsheets, multiple apps, and manual data entry, Midday can help.

## What can you do with Midday?

### Connect your bank accounts

Link your business bank accounts to automatically sync transactions. Midday supports over 20,000 banks worldwide through secure connections. Your transactions flow in automatically, categorized by AI.

[Learn how to connect your bank →](/docs/connect-bank-account)

### Send professional invoices

Create and send invoices that look great on any device. Set up recurring invoices for retainer clients, schedule delivery in advance, and accept online payments via Stripe.

[Learn how to create invoices →](/docs/create-invoice)

### Track your time

Use the built-in timer or log hours manually. Organize work by project and client. When you're ready, turn tracked time into invoices with one click.

[Learn how to track time →](/docs/track-time-timer)

### Match receipts automatically

Forward receipts to your Midday inbox or connect Gmail/Outlook. Our AI matches documents to transactions automatically—no more manual filing.

[Learn how receipt matching works →](/docs/receipt-matching)

### Understand your finances

See your revenue, profit, burn rate, and runway at a glance. Midday calculates these in real-time based on your actual transactions.

[Learn about reports →](/docs/view-revenue-profit)

### Export for your accountant

When it's time for taxes or year-end, export clean, categorized transactions to CSV or sync directly to Xero, QuickBooks, or Fortnox.

[Learn how to export →](/docs/export-transactions-csv)
