Share financial reports with stakeholders who need visibility into your business performance.

## Share a report

1. Go to [Overview](https://app.midday.ai) or the specific report you want to share
2. Click **Share**
3. Choose sharing method:
   - **Link**: Generate a shareable URL
   - **PDF**: Download to send manually
   - **Email**: Send directly from Midday

## Shareable link

Generate a link that anyone can view:

- Link is private by default (only people with the link can access)
- Optional password protection
- Set expiration date

### Link settings

- **Password**: Require a password to view
- **Expires**: Auto-disable after a date
- **Permissions**: View only (no editing)

## PDF export

Download a PDF for:

- Sending via your own email
- Printing for meetings
- Archiving for records

PDFs include:

- Report data as of export time
- Your business branding
- Date range and filters applied

## What you can share

### Overview dashboard

High-level metrics:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Burn rate
- Runway

### Specific reports

- Revenue by period
- Expense breakdown
- Profit and loss
- Cash flow

## Managing shared links

View and manage your shared reports:

1. Go to [Settings](https://app.midday.ai/settings)
2. See all active shared links
3. Revoke or update as needed

## Use cases

### For investors

Monthly or quarterly overview showing:

- Revenue growth
- Burn rate
- Runway

### For accountants

Detailed transaction exports and reports:

- Categorized transactions
- Profit and loss
- Use [direct integrations](/docs/connect-xero) for automated sync

### For partners

Specific project reports:

- Time tracked
- Revenue generated
- Outstanding invoices

## Tips

- Set expiration dates for sensitive reports
- Use passwords for extra security
- Review shared links periodically and revoke unused ones
