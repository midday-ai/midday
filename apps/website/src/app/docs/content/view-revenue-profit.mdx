Understanding your revenue and profit helps you make informed business decisions.

## Access financial reports

1. Go to [Overview](https://app.midday.ai)

You'll see cards showing your key metrics.

## Revenue

Revenue is the total money coming in:

- Invoice payments
- Sales
- Other income transactions

### View revenue

- Current period total
- Comparison to previous period
- Trend over time

Revenue is based on **income** transactions in your connected accounts.

## Profit

Profit is revenue minus expenses:

- **Revenue**: All income
- **Expenses**: All outgoing transactions (excluding transfers)
- **Profit**: What's left

### View profit

- Current period profit
- Profit margin (profit as % of revenue)
- Trend over time

## Date ranges

Switch between:

- This month / Last month
- This quarter / Last quarter
- This year / Last year
- Custom date range

## Filters

Filter to see specific views:

- By account (see profit from a specific bank account)
- By category (see revenue from certain categories)

## Share reports

1. Open the report you want to share
2. Click **Export** or **Share**
3. Get a link or download PDF

## Tips

- Review weekly to catch issues early
- Compare periods to spot trends
