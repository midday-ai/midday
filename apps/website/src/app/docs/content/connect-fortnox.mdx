Connect Midday to Fortnox to automatically sync your categorized transactions. Popular with Swedish businesses using Fortnox for accounting.

## How it works

Once connected:

1. Transactions in Midday sync to Fortnox automatically
2. Categories map to your Fortnox accounts
3. Your accountant sees clean, pre-categorized data

No more manual data entry or file exports.

## Connect Fortnox

1. Go to [Apps](https://app.midday.ai/apps)
2. Find **Fortnox**
3. Click **Connect**
4. Sign in to your Fortnox account
5. Authorize Midday

## Category mapping

Midday categories map to Fortnox account codes:

- Match each Midday category to the appropriate Fortnox account
- Follows Swedish BAS accounting standards
- You can update mappings in settings

## What syncs

- Transaction date
- Amount
- Description
- Category (mapped to Fortnox account)
- Attached receipts (as files)

## Sync frequency

Transactions sync automatically:

- New transactions are pushed when they're categorized
- You can also trigger a manual sync

## Disconnect

To stop syncing:

1. Go to [Apps](https://app.midday.ai/apps)
2. Click **Disconnect** on Fortnox

Past synced data remains in Fortnox.
