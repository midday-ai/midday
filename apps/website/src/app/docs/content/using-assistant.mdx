The Midday assistant lets you ask questions about your finances in plain language and get instant answers.

## Access the assistant

1. Open [Midday](https://app.midday.ai)
2. The assistant is available from the home screen
3. Type your question
4. Get an instant response

## What you can ask

The assistant understands questions about your financial data:

### Revenue and income
- "What was my revenue last month?"
- "Show me revenue by customer this quarter"
- "How does this month compare to last month?"

### Expenses and spending
- "What did I spend on software last quarter?"
- "Show my top expenses this year"
- "How much have I spent with Amazon?"

### Cash and runway
- "What's my current balance?"
- "How long will my cash last?"
- "What's my monthly burn rate?"

### Invoices
- "Do I have any overdue invoices?"
- "Who owes me money?"
- "Show unpaid invoices for Acme Corp"

### Time tracking
- "How many hours did I track this week?"
- "Show time by project this month"
- "What's unbilled for Client X?"

## How it works

The assistant:

1. Understands your question using AI
2. Queries your actual Midday data
3. Returns accurate, real-time answers

## Tips

- Be specific: "Revenue last month" is better than "How am I doing?"
- Include time periods: "this month," "last quarter," "in 2024"
- Ask follow-ups: The assistant remembers context in a conversation

## Limitations

The assistant can answer questions about data in Midday. It cannot:

- Make predictions or forecasts
- Access external data
- Make changes to your account (it's read-only)
- Answer questions about features not yet available

## Privacy

Your conversations with the assistant are private. We don't use your financial data to train AI models.
