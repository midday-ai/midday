The Midday assistant lets you ask questions, take actions, and manage your business in plain language — directly from the dashboard.

## Access the assistant

1. Open [Midday](https://app.midday.ai)
2. The assistant is available from the home screen
3. Type your question or request
4. Get an instant response — or watch the assistant take action for you

## Chat modes

The assistant supports three modes you can switch between:

- **Auto** — The default. Automatically picks the best approach for your question — fast for simple lookups, more thorough for complex analysis.
- **Instant** — Optimized for speed. Best for quick lookups like "show my balance" or "list overdue invoices".
- **Thinking** — Deep reasoning mode for complex questions like financial comparisons, forecasts, or multi-step analysis. Available on Pro plans.

## What you can do

The assistant can both answer questions and take real actions on your behalf.

### Ask about your finances

- "What was my revenue last month?"
- "Show me revenue by customer this quarter"
- "How does this month compare to last month?"
- "What did I spend on software last quarter?"
- "What's my current balance?"
- "How long will my cash last?"

### Create and manage invoices

- "Create an invoice for Acme Corp for 10 hours of consulting at $150/hr"
- "Send the draft invoice to Acme Corp"
- "Remind Beta Inc about their overdue invoice"
- "Mark invoice INV-0042 as paid"
- "What invoices are overdue?"

### Manage transactions

- "Categorize all my Stripe transactions as income"
- "Tag these transactions as tax-deductible"
- "Delete the manual transaction from yesterday"
- "Show transactions from last month"

### Track time

- "Start a timer on Project X"
- "Log 2 hours for the client meeting yesterday"
- "How many hours did I track this week?"
- "What's unbilled for Project X?"

### Export and report

- "Export my January transactions to my accountant at lisa@accounting.com"
- "What's my growth rate compared to last quarter?"
- "Give me a balance sheet as of today"
- "Show spending by category for last month"

## Web search

The assistant can search the web for real-time information and combine it with your business data:

- "Can I afford a Tesla Model 3?" — looks up the current price and checks your balance or runway.
- "What's the VAT rate in Germany?" — finds the rate and checks your relevant transactions.
- "How does my revenue compare to industry average?" — searches for benchmarks and pulls your actual numbers.

This is useful for questions involving prices, exchange rates, tax rules, or industry benchmarks.

## Connected apps

The assistant can interact with external tools you've connected — Gmail, Slack, Google Calendar, Notion, GitHub, Linear, and more.

- "Check my calendar for tomorrow"
- "Send a Slack message to the team channel"
- "Look up the latest GitHub issue on our repo"
- "Create a Notion page with this month's revenue summary"

Connect apps from the **Connectors** settings in your dashboard. See [Connected Apps](/docs/assistant-connectors) for the full list and setup instructions.

## Tips

- **Be specific**: "Revenue last month" works better than "How am I doing?"
- **Include time periods**: "this month," "last quarter," "in 2025"
- **Ask follow-ups**: The assistant remembers context within a conversation
- **Combine actions**: "Create an invoice for Acme Corp and send it" works in one go
- **Use natural language**: You don't need special syntax — just describe what you want

## Limitations

The assistant cannot:

- Send emails (other than invoice send/remind)
- Connect bank accounts or modify account settings
- Manage billing or subscriptions
- Upload files

For these, use the corresponding pages in the Midday dashboard.

## Privacy

Your conversations with the assistant are private. We don't use your financial data or conversation history to train AI models.
