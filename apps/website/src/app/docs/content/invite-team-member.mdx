Invite colleagues to your Midday team so they can help manage finances, track time, or create invoices.

## Invite a team member

1. Go to [Settings → Members](https://app.midday.ai/settings/members)
2. Click **Invite**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role
5. Send the invitation

They'll receive an email with instructions to join your team.

## Roles

### Owner

Full access to everything:

- Manage billing and subscription
- Invite and remove members
- Access all financial data
- Delete the team

There's always at least one owner.

### Member

Access to day-to-day features:

- View transactions
- Create and send invoices
- Track time
- View reports

Cannot manage team settings or billing.

## Pending invitations

View outstanding invitations:

1. Go to [Settings → Members](https://app.midday.ai/settings/members)
2. See pending invites
3. Resend or cancel if needed

Invitations expire after 7 days.

## Remove a member

1. Go to [Settings → Members](https://app.midday.ai/settings/members)
2. Find the member
3. Click **Remove**
4. Confirm

Removed members immediately lose access. Their past activity (time entries, invoices created) remains.

## Change a role

1. Go to [Settings → Members](https://app.midday.ai/settings/members)
2. Find the member
3. Click to change their role
4. Save

## Tips

- Keep the number of owners limited
- Use member roles for day-to-day users
- Review member access periodically
